Yang Yongyi(杨永祎)

Yongyi is a senior student at Fudan University, under supervision of Prof. Qiu Xipeng. Currently he is having an internship at Amazon Shanghai AI Lab and has his fortune to be advised by Dr. David Wipf and Prof. Huang Zengfeng.

His recent research interests include graph representation learning and optimization-induced models.

He is applying for Ph.D. study recently.
